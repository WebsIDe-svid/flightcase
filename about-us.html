Meet the committee

Willem Borsboom

Chair

Willem is a Design for Interaction student who focuses on making (new) technologies fulfill human needs. With a human-centered approach and a strong understanding of context, he ensures that designs integrate seamlessly into their environment. As chair of Flight Case, he helps the team members to display their individual strengths. Willem believes that working with multicultural and multidisciplinary teams leads to creative and out-of-the-box solutions by bringing in diverse perspectives.

This year's professors

Dr. R. (Roy) Bendor

Roy Bendor is Associate Professor of Critical Design in the Department of Human-Centered Design at Delft University of Technology, the Netherlands, where he also directs the Design for Interaction (DfI) Master’s program. His research explores the capacity of design to disclose alternative social, political and environmental futures. Roy is also Fellow of the Urban Futures Studio at Utrecht University, former editor of the sustainability forum in the ACM’s magazine Interactions, and author of Interactive Media for Sustainability (Palgrave, 2018). Find out more about him on his website www.digitalsustainability.com
