Your next adventure

During this summer, 28 master students and 2 professors will go to Japan to work on cases for inspiring companies, experience the local culture and much more!

The aim of Flight Case is to create an experience that connects an international team of qualified master students with leading companies all over the world. It will allow you as a student to build up an international network and experience working in another culture.

We will 'work hard', but also 'play hard'. We want to show you the contrasts: city vs. nature, the global vs. the local, and more. We aim to provide you with an experience you will never forget!

Applications are closed

What we offer

Work with real companies

Flight Case will enable you to discover your skills and put your knowledge into practice with real companies.

Dream team and professional network

You will form a design agency with 27 other students and 2 professors. This is the time to build a professional network that lasts longer than just this trip.

Experience another culture

During Flight Case, you will immerse yourself in a new lifestyle and experience a different view on design and life habits.

6 ECTS

You will receive 6 ECTS for participating in Flight Case. This includes taking part in Flight Course in Q4. Flight Case is one of the faculty electives you can take.

FAQs

How many students can go on Flight Case?

+

From all submissions, we will choose 20 students to join Flight Case. Together with our eight committee members, that means 28 students will go on the trip, accompanied by 2 professors from IDE.

Who can join Flight Case?

+

You can join Flight Case if you are enrolled in one of the three masters of IDE at TU Delft. You also need to be enrolled to one of the masters during the application.

How will you choose the participants?

+

We will choose the participants based on their motivation, CV and portfolio. We aim to create a diverse team with a mix of master disciplines and nationalities.

When will I know if I can participate?

+

You should hear back from us around the last week of January or the first week of February. It will take us some time process the applications. If you get accepted, you have one week to decide if you want to accept your place.

Is travel insurance included in the application fee?

+

No, you are responsible for your own insurances (e.g. travel, health, luggage, accident insurance).

What is Flight Course?

+

In Q4, Flight Course is an elective to prepare the cases for the trip. The elective will be scheduled on the Mondays of Q4. On Mondays, there are no other electives. Attending Flight Course is mandatory to join Flight Case and to get your 6 ECTs.

What companies will we be working with?

+

We are looking for diverse companies that can provide us with different cases that will fit all master's programs. To get a better idea of the type of cases we'll be doing, take a look at past experiences.

What happens if I decide not to go on Flight Case?

+

In case a participant withdraws from Flight Case after signing the contract, the participant is obliged to pay the costs which are caused by withdrawing from Flight Case.

When will we know the exact dates of Flight Case?

+

We are planning on buying the flight tickets in March. That is the moment when we will know the exact flying dates. For now, you need to keep the summer planning between July 10th and August 10th free.

What are our ethical considerations for choosing the companies?

+

When selecting companies for our cases, we adhere to an internal policy that prioritizes ethical and sustainable considerations. We aim to collaborate with organizations that align with our values, ensuring that the cases we work on reflect responsible practices and contribute positively to society.
