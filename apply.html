What is in my application?

When applying for Flight Case 2025, you are asked to hand in a motivation letter, your CV and your portfolio

Motivation letter

+

Write a motivation letter in which you at least answer the following questions:

- Why do you want to join Flight Case?
- What will you bring to Flight Case?

Maximum of 350 words

File name format:
motivation_studentnumber.pdf

CV

+

In your CV you can include your education, work experience, committees, internships, skills etc.

Max 1 A4

File name format:
cv_studentnumber.pdf

Portfolio

+

Your portfolio should include:

- 1 page about who you are as a designer.
- A maximum of 3 projects

Make sure everything you want to tell is in the 8 pages. Don’t link to things outside the PDF, such as videos.

To have a fair way of comparing portfolios, we have standardised the documents to be a PDF. Unfortunately, we do not accept website portfolios.

Max 8 pages

File name format:
portfolio_studentnumber.pdf

Extra: 'Fun' question

+

In the application form, we included a ‘fun’ question:

- Describe what kind of sushi you are.
- Use this description as a prompt to make an AI image. (eg. with Midjourney or ChatGPT)
- Upload this image to the form.

Anonymize your application

We will make the participants selection by judging anonymous applications. Make sure to remove your name, gender and nationality from the deliverables. Instead, include your student number.
