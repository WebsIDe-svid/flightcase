What are the parts of the application?

Application details

+

Fill in basic applicant details: student number, gender, nationality, and master track

FlightCase application

+

Using the Canva or Figma templates provided, fill in the FlightCase application and upload as a PDF to the application form. This is the core of the application process where you can express your personality and creativity.

Curriculum Vitae (CV)

+

Upload an anonymized C as a PDF which provides some context about you professionally. While professional experience is important, this will be the least weighted portion of the application.

Anonymize your application

We will make the participants selection by judging anonymous applications. Make sure to remove your name, gender and nationality from the deliverables. Instead, include your student number.
